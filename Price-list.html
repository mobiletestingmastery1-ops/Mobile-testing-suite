‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How quickly can you come to my location?

We offer same-day service in most areas (additional fee applies). Standard appointments are typically available within 24-48 hours.

Q: What areas do you service?

We provide mobile services within a 50-mile radius of our location. Travel fees apply for distances over 15 miles. Contact us for service availability in your area.

Q: Do I need an appointment?

Yes, all services require an appointment to ensure proper scheduling and preparation. Call, text, or book online.

Q: Are your results legally admissible?

Yes! When you select legal/court-admissible testing, we follow strict chain of custody protocols that meet all court requirements. Our DNA and drug testing results are accepted nationwide.

Q: How long until I get my results?

Results timing varies by test type. Most drug screens: 24-48 hours. DNA tests: 3-7 business days. Blood work: 2-5 days depending on panel. Rush processing is available for most tests.

Q: What do I need to bring?

Valid photo ID is required for all services. For physician-ordered tests, bring your lab requisition form. For legal testing, all parties must present government-issued photo ID.

Q: Do you offer group rates?

Yes! We provide discounted pricing for corporate clients, law firms, and organizations requiring multiple tests. Contact us for a custom quote based on your volume and frequency needs.

Q: Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. We are HIPAA compliant and take client confidentiality very seriously. All results are delivered securely and only to authorized parties.
